Projects

I've had the opportunity to build and deploy end-to-end data analytics solutions, leveraging tools like Python, SQL, Tableau, and cloud platforms such as AWS and Snowflake. One of my proudest achievements was creating an automated pipeline to analyze over 420,000 vehicle records, helping uncover trends in sales, pricing, and inventory, which directly contributed to better BI reporting. Additionally, I worked with hospital billing and readmission data, optimizing SQL queries with advanced techniques like CTEs and window functions to provide valuable insights on performance and patient demographics.
I also developed Tableau dashboards that explored large datasets, such as over 140,000 Spotify listening logs and nearly 280,000 Airbnb listings, uncovering patterns in user behavior, platform engagement, and pricing. My work also involved conducting exploratory data analysis (EDA) on sales data to assess the impact of COVID-19, and I optimized SQL queries to identify top-performing books by author, genre, and sales trends over time.
Throughout these projects, I focused on cleaning and modeling data, building scalable workflows, and using data visualization to tell meaningful stories that drive insights and decision-making.

About Me

Hey, I’m Saiyam Masalia, a curious and data-driven professional with a Master’s in Computer Science from Oregon State University. I’m passionate about transforming raw, messy data into clear, meaningful insights that help businesses work smarter. I’ve worked with tools like SQL, Python, Excel, and Tableau to analyze data in domains like healthcare, finance, sales, and Airbnb listings. Whether it’s cleaning and structuring data, building dashboards, or creating end-to-end reporting pipelines, I enjoy crafting solutions that improve employee productivity, business decisions, and the user experience. Recently, I wrapped up a project on vehicle data analysis, and I’m now expanding my skills into the world of analytics engineering — exploring tools like dbt, Snowflake, Azure, Databricks, and AWS to build scalable data workflows and models.

I’m looking for opportunities where I can grow as a Data Analyst or Analytics Engineer, contribute to impactful work, and keep learning from great teams. Outside of work, I’m into anime, cricket, and the occasional deep dive into a good coffee or coding session.
